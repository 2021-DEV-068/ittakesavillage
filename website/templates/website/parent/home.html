ARE YOU PREGNANT? ARE YOU A PARENT?

FIND HELP AND SUPPORT

Are you about to have a baby? Or maybe your baby is already born? Do you have a million questions? Do you often feel overwhelmed? Pregnancy, birth and post-partum are life changing moments in the life of a couple and at BearHug we believe that the transition into parenthood should be an easy and peaceful one. At BearHug you can find help and support to accompany you and your partner through the journey of parenthood. Because after all, it takes a village to raise a child!



1 SIGN UP

Sign up to create your online account to keep track of your bookings.

2 CHOOSE THE SUPPORT YOU NEED

Find the help you are seeking! Thanks to our category drop down menu, you can select the field in which you are seeking support. All the professions offering help in that category will show up. There is much more help available out there than parents often know. Help is on the way!

3 BOOK YOUR SESSION

Once you have found your caregiver, you can easily book an appointment. You will get free reminders before your appointments, so you don’t have to think about it anymore!



HOW BEARHUG CAN HELP YOU?

Help and Support!

Becoming a parent can be quite a transition. And at BearHug we believe that this transition into parenthood should be a serene and peaceful experience. At BearHug you can find support and get early childhood professionals to guide you from pregnancy all the way through post-partum. They will listen, help and support you. Pregnancy and parenthood can often be unchartered territories, which it doesn’t need to be. We want to enable you to make informed decisions about childbirth, your post-partum wellbeing, your relationship and parenthood. Knowledge is power and we strive to help you find yours!

Passionate and Qualified Professionals

All our professionals are people who do what they love. Supporting parents transitioning into parenthood is their calling. They are passionate, competent and carrying. The profiles of all the caregivers have been thoroughly verified by us personally. Recommendations by other parents, help you and other to make informed decisions.

Baby Care and Parental Care

Whether you are looking for support for your baby or for you or for you and your partner, you can find it! Being a parent is also about taking care of yourself and your relationship. The better you feel and the happier you are, the more you have to offer. We care about the whole family’s wellbeing!

Book face-to-face or online sessions

Post-partum can also be a time for cocooning and privacy, which we understand and respect. Therefore, you can choose whether you want a physical or an online consultation with your profession caregiver.

People who care about you!

At BearHug we believe in community and supporting each other! You are not alone! Help is on the way! Because, after all, raising a child takes a village!
